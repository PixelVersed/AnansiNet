# Contributing to AnansiNet

Thank you for your interest in contributing to **AnansiNet**!  
We welcome pull requests, bug reports, and feature suggestions from the community.

---

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork** the repository and create a new branch for your changes.  
2. **Write clear, documented code** and include tests when possible.  
3. **Submit a pull request** to the `main` branch with a clear description of your changes.  
4. Ensure your code follows the project’s coding style and passes all checks.

---

## Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

Before we can accept your contribution, you must agree to our **Contributor License Agreement (CLA)**.

By submitting a pull request, you confirm that:
- You have read and agreed to the [CLA](./CLA.md).  
- You have the legal right to contribute this code.  
- You understand that your contribution may be relicensed by the project owner under different terms, including proprietary ones.

If you’re contributing through GitHub:
- You’ll be prompted to agree to the CLA automatically via a CLA bot, **or**
- You can indicate agreement by adding this line to your commit message:

```
Signed-off-by: Your Name your.email@example.com
```

---

## Code of Conduct
Please be respectful and follow our [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) when participating in the community.
